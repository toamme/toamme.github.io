Bio

Since October 2023, I am a first-year PhD student under supervision of Mohammad Abdulaziz at King's College London. Before that, I did my Bachelor's (completion in September 2021) and Master's degree (completion in October 2023) at the Technical University of Munich (TUM).

My interests are the formalisation of algorithms for combinatorial optimisation problems in Isabelle.
